Medium monstrosity, neutral evil

Hit Points 33 (6d8 + 6)
Speed 40 ft.
str dex con int wis cha

Proficiency Bonus +2
Charge
. If the doom boar moves at least 20 feet straight
toward a target and then hits it with a tusk attack on
the same turn, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) slashing
damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a
DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
Reckless
. At the start of its turn, the doom boar can
gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during
that turn, but attack rolls against it have advantage until
the start of its next turn.
Two-Headed
. The doom boar has advantage on Wisdom
(Perception) checks and on saving throws against being
blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened, stunned, or
knocked unconscious.
actions
Multiattack
. The doom boar makes two tusk attacks.

.
