Augmented Movement.
The garish augment has two
other movement modes selected from: burrow 10 feet, climb 30 feet, and swim 30 feet.
Augmented Spit.
The garish augment deals acid,
necrotic or poison damage with its spit (choose one).
Being within 5 ft. of a hostile creature doesn’t cause the
garish augment to have disadvantage on the attack roll.
Augmented Strikes.
The garish augment deals
bludgeoning, slashing, or piercing damage with its
strike (choose one).
Jumper.
The garish augment jumps twice as far as its
ability check to do so indicates.
Unnatural Presence.
Effects that detect aberrations or
fiends can detect the garish augment.
