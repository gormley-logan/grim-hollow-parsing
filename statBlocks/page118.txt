Faevlin
Small fey, neutral evil
Armor Class 15 (leather armor, shield)
Hit Points 10 (3d6)
Speed 30 ft.
str dex con int wis cha
 8 (–1) 14 (+2) 10 (+0) 10 (+0) 12 (+1) 11 (+0)
Skills
Stealth +4
Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
Languages Goblin, Sylvan
Challenge 1/4 (50 XP)
Proficiency Bonus +2
Fey Touched.
Magic can’t put the faevlin to sleep.
Only a creature of the fey type can cause the faevlin to
become charmed, and a faevlin always knows if a nonfey
creature attempted to render it charmed.
Sneaky.
The faevlin can use a bonus action to take the
Hide action.
actions
Scimitar.
Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft.,
one target.
Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) slashing damage.
Shortbow.
Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range
80/320 ft., one target.
Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
reactions
Fey Fade.
When an attack misses a faevlin, the faevlin
can teleport up to 30 feet in a random direction, arriving
in a safe, unoccupied space.
