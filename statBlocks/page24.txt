Bounce.
When using the Dash action, the bladewhirl
can high jump a distance up to its current speed. It takes
no falling damage from this jump.
Grappled Menace.
A bladewhirl can’t grapple, but
it has advantage on attack rolls against any creature
grappling it.
Indiscriminate.
Whenever the bladewhirl starts its
turn, it attacks the closest creature it can sense. If no crea-
ture is near enough to move to and attack, the bladewhirl
takes the Dash action to move toward the nearest crea-
ture or moves in a random direction seeking a creature.
