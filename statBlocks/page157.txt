Amphibious.
The horror flit swarm can breathe air
and water.
Breed.
When the horror flit swarm kills a Small or
larger creature, a new horror flit swarm emerges from
the corpse 1d4 hours later.
Spider Climb.
The horror flit swarm can climb difficult
surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without
needing to make an ability check.
Swarm.
The horror flit swarm can occupy another
creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move
through any opening large enough for a Tiny creature. The
swarm can’t regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.
